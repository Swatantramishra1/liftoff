Accern

Completed November 2016
Client Accern

Accern was born in late 2013 out of a desire to automate the discovery of early financial news. We wanted to eliminate the manual process of going to multiple news websites to find actionable news story. Furthermore, we wanted to eliminate the cost of clients subscribing to multiple news platforms and data feeds. By building upon this concept, we rapidly grew to covering over 300 million public news websites, blogs, social media websites such as Twitter, and public financial documents such as SEC filings. With a skilled team of PhDs and computer engineers, we have positioned ourselves at No.1 in finance news sentiment in terms of our web coverage.

Project Title

Completed August 2016
Client RightFoot.in

Innovative institute of mathematics (IIM Jaipur) is a pioneer institute for mathematics for all higher level competitive exams like CSIR-NET, GATE, IIT-JAM, First Grade Teachers, M.Sc. entrance exams like TIFR, ISI, CMI, NBHM etc. in Jaipur. It was founded just two years back in 2014 and in this short span of time it has shown its presence and is established as a national level institute for higher mathematics now. IIM Jaipur believes in imparting mathematical concepts and skills to its students at core level and hence it has started courses for graduation mathematics. “A good start is half work done.” IIM Jaipur has started its first IIT-JAM batch with 6 students and 5 of them are pursuing there M.Sc. from IITs. We had about 70 students in M.Sc. Entrance batch and at least 45 are selected in various universities like RU, CU, DU, BHU etc. It was believed that one must go to Delhi to prepare for exams like CSIR-NET and TIFR but IIM Jaipur has proved it wrong by giving selection in TIFR and CSIR-NET and continuing to do so.
